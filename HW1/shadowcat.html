Shadowcat

Shadowcat was the youngest member to join the X-Men. She became a member of the second incarnation of the team when she was 13. She's been a member of multiple teams including:

  • The X-Men
  • Excalibur
  • S.H.I.E.L.D.
  • New Mutants
  • Guardians of the Galaxy
